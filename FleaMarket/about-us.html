Some Words About Us

We are currently final year CS stundets at UNNC. We find there is an increasing trend at UNNC of trading second-hand textbooks and commodities. Motived by this, we set up this website by ourselves, aiming at providing sellers an online platform to post information and bring convenience to buyers. The information on the webiste is updated at all times. The optimal goal of this website is to equip UNNCers an idea about using Information Technology to perfect our daily life.

We do not charge any service fee, and do not provide any online-trading system. We only provide a way for the UNNCers to exchange information on second-hand goods .

该网站为CS大四学长学姐所建，旨在为UNNCer提供一个二手物品信息分享平台。物品信息时刻更新，欲购从速。

我们愿意免费为您刊登商品信息，请将物品名称，原价，现价，介绍和图片整合成文，联系微信：Autofail。

本网站的初衷是变废为宝，尽可能减少资源浪费，利用互联网手段提高生活质量，给卖家带来利益回报，给买家送去便利快捷。不存在任何竞争关系与盈利目的（仅卖家获利）。
